Submission

Ara Korea International Film Festival (AKIFF) accepts film submissions exclusively through FilmFreeway.

Submit via FilmFreeway

General Information

  • AKIFF is an offline, in-person international film festival held in Korea.
  • All films are reviewed by the festival programming team.
  • Selected films will be screened at the official festival venue.

Eligibility

  • Open to filmmakers worldwide.
  • All genres and formats are welcome.
  • Films must be submitted with English information.

Selection & Screening

  • Official selections will be announced on FilmFreeway.
  • Selected filmmakers will be contacted directly by the festival.
  • Screening details will be provided after venue confirmation.
