Ara Korea International Film Festival

About AKIFF

An offline international film festival in Korea, dedicated to discovering and supporting emerging filmmakers worldwide.

Identity

Ara Korea International Film Festival (AKIFF) is an in-person festival based in Korea. We curate screenings and an awards program that highlight new cinematic voices across genres and formats.

Vision

AKIFF exists to strengthen the connection between filmmakers and audiences through a focused, well-curated program. We aim to provide clear visibility for selected works and to support filmmakers at an early stage of their career.

Focus

  • Emerging filmmakers and bold storytelling
  • Artistic quality and cinematic craft
  • In-person screenings and audience experience
  • International submissions and fair evaluation

At a glance

  • Format: Offline / in-person screenings
  • Location: Korea (Venue TBA)
  • Language: English (official communication)
  • Submissions: FilmFreeway only
